Resolution Confidence Score

A pre-trade signal exposing oracle and dispute risk — absent from native Polymarket / Kalshi UIs.

RESOLUTION CONFIDENCE SCORE / 0–100

100 × (0.40 · Clarity + 0.35 · Evidence + 0.25 · (1−Risk))

40%

Criteria Clarity

Date anchor, threshold, canonical wording.

35%

Evidence Consensus

Multi-source corroboration, weighted by authority.

25%

Integrity Risk

Inverted: liquidity, volume, dispute priors.

Worked example: "Will Bitcoin hit $1M before GTA VI?" scores ~70 — clean criteria but elevated dispute risk on the conjunction. Size accordingly.
